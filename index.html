How verification works

A credential you can trust, checked in seconds

Every diploma and transcript HCDC issues is hashed with SHA-256 and anchored on-chain. Uploading a document or scanning its QR code re-computes the hash and compares it against the ledger — no phone calls to the registrar required.

  • Registrar approves the credential and generates a SHA-256 hash.
  • The hash is written to the blockchain with a transaction ID.
  • A QR code is generated and attached to the digital document.
  • Anyone can scan or upload the file to confirm authenticity.
